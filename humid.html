vs. Humidity

Although Latitude vs. Humidity does not show clear correlation the further analysis based on the Hemispheres show weak correlation. In North Hemisphere, closer to the equator the less the humidity. There is less data for the South Hemisphere since for the most part it is covered with Ocean but from the available data we can see that the humidity is more or less identical in both Hemispheres with some outliers between Lat [-20, -40]
